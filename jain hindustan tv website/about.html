Who We Are

Jain Hindustan News TV, RNI Certified (RAJAIN/2022/88128), stands as one of India's leading YouTube news channels, delivering insightful and comprehensive coverage of national and international affairs. Our platform is dedicated to keeping viewers informed with authentic, timely, and impactful journalism. Beyond breaking news, we offer a diverse spectrum of content, including exclusive interviews, in-depth series, political analysis, social issues, religious and mythological narratives. Our mission is to uphold the integrity of journalism while bringing you stories that matter. With a commitment to truth and transparency, Jain Hindustan News TV is your trusted window to the world.

Cheif of JHN TV

Shri Virendra Abhani - A Visionary in Journalism & Social Service Shri Virendra Abhani is a renowned journalist, writer, singer, social worker, and filmmaker. Originally from Bikaner, he began his career in Mumbai, working with Shri Balasaheb Thackeray at Saamana newspaper. He later collaborated with Ram Gopal Varma in the film industry and produced the impactful movie "Ye Kaisa Hai Dahej". As the founder of Jain Hindustan TV, he upholds fearless and unbiased journalism. During the COVID-19 pandemic, he prioritized social service, ensuring vital information reached the public. A devotional singer and social activist, he actively supports environmental conservation, animal welfare, and mentoring young journalists. With a commitment to truth and social upliftment, he continues to inspire and lead in media and beyond.

Shri Rajesh Acharya - Associate Editor Rajesh Acharya is a dedicated journalist with a keen eye for truth and accuracy. As the Associate Editor of Jain Hindustan TV, he plays a vital role in maintaining the channel's commitment to unbiased and impactful news reporting.
